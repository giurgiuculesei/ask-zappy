Question 1

[Maximum mark: 4]

Expand (2x + 1)4 in descending powers of x and simplify your answer.

even more text bal vla

Mark Scheme

Method (Binomial Theorem):

\[ (a+b)^4=\binom{4}{0}a^4+\binom{4}{1}a^3b+\binom{4}{2}a^2b^2+\binom{4}{3}ab^3+\binom{4}{4}b^4 \]

Let \(a=2x\), \(b=1\): \((2x+1)^4=16x^4+32x^3+24x^2+8x+1\).

Answer: \(\boxed{16x^4 + 32x^3 + 24x^2 + 8x + 1}\)
